### Atlas Messages

In Atlas Messages, you can send direct messages, create servers, and update both your clients and team simultaneously using the server-based messaging system.

### Workspace Settings

When you open the workspace settings, the following options are available:

- **Server Profile** – View and edit the server’s details.
- **Roles** – Manage user roles and permissions.
- **Members** – View and manage all members of the server.
- **Invites** – Create and manage invite links to your server.
- **Channels** – Add, edit, or remove server channels.
- **Delete Server** – Permanently delete the server.
- **Close** – Exit the settings panel.
