_After entering the Contracts portal, you will see the following page._

![Screenshot 2025-08-19 141109.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-19141109.png)

**Step 1: Upload your first contract**\
Click **Upload Contract** in the top-right corner. You can either drag and drop your document or browse to select the file from your computer.

![Screenshot 2025-08-19 143705.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-19143705.png)

**Step 2: Edit your contract**\
After uploading, click on the contract you wish to edit. You will be taken to the **Recipients** and **Fields** sections.

- Add the email addresses of the signees. You can include one or multiple recipients as needed. _(The image on the right shows the Recipients section for reference.)_

![Screenshot 2025-08-19 145721.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-19145721.png)

- **Fields**: Define the required fields (e.g., signature, date, text fields) that recipients must complete. (the image below displays recipients and field section)
